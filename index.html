Interactive Tourism Analysis Dashboard

Understanding Data Units:

  • Tourism Exports: Tourism services exported (USD millions)
  • Tourism Departures: People traveling abroad from residence country
  • Tourism Expenditures: Resident spending abroad (USD millions)
  • Inflation: Annual price change (percentage)
  • Unemployment: Unemployed labor force (percentage)
  • Tourism Receipts: International tourism revenue (USD)
  • Bubble Size: Tourism arrivals (visitor numbers)
  • Bubble Color: GDP in USD (darker = higher)
